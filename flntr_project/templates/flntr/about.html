About flntr

flntr is a web application connecting students in need of a room with landlords in need of tenants in Glasgow.

flntr is ideal for students who can’t or don’t want to find flatmates before looking for a flat. They can view flats as well as the students who are already signed up to each flat.

fltnr makes the whole process easier for landlords as they no longer have to wait for groups of students to fill their flat. Instead, students simply apply to join a flat as individuals.
